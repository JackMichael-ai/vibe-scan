ISO 10816-3 Velocity Thresholds (mm/s RMS)

International standard for evaluating vibration severity in rotating machinery. VibeScan reports in mm/s velocity so these values apply directly — no conversion needed.

Why mm/s not m/s²?

Accelerometers measure acceleration. But material fatigue in bearings and shafts correlates better with velocity — how fast the surface moves, not how hard it accelerates. The C++ engine integrates each FFT bin (divide by 2πf) to convert. This is what makes VibeScan a professional tool rather than a raw sensor readout.

Real sample rate detection

Android is not a real-time OS. SENSOR_DELAY_FASTEST delivers samples at ~185–215Hz depending on the phone model, CPU load, and temperature. VibeScan computes the actual rate from hardware timestamps every window — if your Samsung delivers 191Hz, your BPFO peak appears at the right frequency, not shifted by 4.5%.
